## [‚Äã](https://docs.omi.me/doc/developer/apps/Introduction\#what-are-omi-apps)  What Are OMI Apps?

OMI apps are modular extensions that augment the core functionality of the app. They can modify app‚Äôs
behavior, analyze conversations, and connect with external services.Apps enable:

- Customized AI personalities and knowledge bases
- Extraction of specific information from conversations
- Task automation and integration with third-party services
- Real-time conversation analysis and insights

Apps allow developers to tap into OMI‚Äôs conversational abilities and combine them with external data and services,
opening up a world of possibilities for AI-enhanced applications.
