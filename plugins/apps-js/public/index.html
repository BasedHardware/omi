How to Use Omi Apps

Your voice assistant provides two powerful apps: Email Assistant for managing emails and Presentation Generator for creating slides.

1. Choose Your App

Start with the appropriate trigger phrase:

  • "Hey Email" - For email operations (send, check, search emails)
  • "Hey Omi" - For presentation generation (create slides, make decks)

2. Email Assistant Commands

Sending Emails

To send an email, you can use phrases like:

"Hey Email, send an email to John Doe, subject Project Update, message Let's discuss the progress on the TPS reports next week."

Or with direct email address: "Hey Email, email jane.doe@example.com about the marketing campaign and tell her the new ad creatives are ready for review."

Fetching Emails

To fetch and read emails, you can use commands like:

  • "Hey Email, check my inbox" - Gets recent emails
  • "Hey Email, any new emails from John?" - Checks emails from a specific person
  • "Hey Email, find emails about the project update" - Searches emails by topic

3. Presentation Generator Commands

Creating Presentations

To generate a presentation, use commands like:

  • "Hey Omi, create a presentation about artificial intelligence trends"
  • "Hey Omi, make slides on quarterly sales results"
  • "Hey Omi, generate a deck about sustainable energy solutions"

3-Notification System

When creating presentations, you'll receive three notifications:

  • 1. Trigger Detection - Immediate response with viewer URL
  • 2. Generation Started - Notification when AI processing begins
  • 3. Completion - Final notification with presentation links or error details

4. Tips for Best Results

General Tips

  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Use the correct trigger phrase for each app ("Hey Email" vs "Hey Omi")
  • Be specific with your requests for better results

Email Assistant Tips

  • Be specific with search criteria when fetching emails
  • You can use natural language like "from yesterday" or "last week" for time-based searches
  • If a name is unusual, spelling it out or providing the full email address can help
  • Use follow-up commands to refine your search results

Presentation Generator Tips

  • Provide detailed topics for better slide content
  • You can access the real-time viewer URL to watch progress
  • Presentations are saved and can be accessed later via embed or download links
  • Wait for all three notifications to ensure completion
