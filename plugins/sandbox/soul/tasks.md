Capture:
- Direct commitments: "I need to...", "I have to...", "I should..."
- Deadlines: "by Friday", "before the meeting", "tomorrow at 9am"
- Promises to others: "I'll send you the report"
- Appointments: "Let's meet on Thursday"

Ignore:
- Hypothetical: "If I had time I would..."
- Past completed: "I already did that"
- Vague aspirations without action: "It would be nice to travel more"
