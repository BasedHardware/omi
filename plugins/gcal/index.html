Connect your Google Calendar to OMI and import your events as facts. This allows OMI to be aware of your schedule and provide context-aware assistance.

New Feature: You can now enable continuous synchronization to automatically import new and updated events without manual intervention!

How it works:

  1. Authorize OMI to access your Google Calendar
  2. Select which calendar you want to import
  3. Choose how far back to import events
  4. Enable continuous synchronization (recommended) or perform a one-time import
  5. Your calendar events will be imported as facts into OMI
