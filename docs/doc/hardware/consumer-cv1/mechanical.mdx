## Device Mechanical Design

The Omi CV1 is a 25.5mm diameter wearable pendant with aluminium covers and a PC+ABS internal frame.

### Full Assembly

- `mechanical/assemblies/cv1-device-charger-assembly.step` — Complete device + charger dock STEP assembly

### 2D Drawings

- `mechanical/drawings/cv1-2d-drawings.pdf` — Technical drawings for all parts
- `mechanical/drawings/manufacturing-specifications.png` — Manufacturing spec overview
